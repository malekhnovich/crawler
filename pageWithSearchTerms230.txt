<p>A <b>Bachelor of Science in Information Technology</b>, (abbreviated <b>BSIT</b> or <b>B.Sc IT</b>), is a <a href="/wiki/Bachelor%27s_degree" title="Bachelor's degree">Bachelor's degree</a> awarded for an <a href="/wiki/Undergraduate_education" title="Undergraduate education">undergraduate</a> course or program in the <a href="/wiki/Information_technology" title="Information technology">Information technology</a> field. The degree is normally required in order to work in the Information technology industry.</p>
<p>A Bachelor of Science in Information Technology degree program typically takes three to four years depending on the country. This degree is primarily focused on subjects such as software, databases, and networking. In general, computer science degrees tend to focus on the mathematical and theoretical foundations of computing rather than emphasizing specific technologies. The degree is a <a href="/wiki/Bachelor_of_Science" title="Bachelor of Science">Bachelor of Science degree</a> with institutions conferring degrees in the fields of information technology and related fields. This degree is awarded for completing a program of study in the field of <a href="/wiki/Software_development" title="Software development">software development</a>, <a href="/wiki/Software_testing" title="Software testing">software testing</a>, <a href="/wiki/Software_engineering" title="Software engineering">software engineering</a>, <a href="/wiki/Web_design" title="Web design">web design</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Databases" title="Databases">databases</a>, <a href="/wiki/Computer_programming" title="Computer programming">programming</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Computer_networking" title="Computer networking">computer networking</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Computer_systems" title="Computer systems">computer systems</a>.</p>
<p>Graduates with an information technology background are able to perform technology tasks relating to the processing, storing, and communication of information between computers, mobile phones, and other electronic devices. Information technology as a field emphasizes the secure management of large amounts of variable information and its accessibility via a wide variety of systems both local and world-wide.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-bls.gov_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-bls.gov-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Skills_taught">Skills taught</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bachelor_of_Science_in_Information_Technology&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Skills taught">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Generally software and information technology companies look for people who have strong programming skills, system analysis, and software testing skills.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>Many universities or colleges teach practical skills that are required to become a software developer. As logical reasoning and critical thinking are important in becoming a software professional, this degree encompasses the complete process of software development from software design and development to final testing.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>Students who complete their undergraduate education in <a href="/wiki/Software_engineering" title="Software engineering">software engineering</a> at a satisfactory level often pursue graduate studies such as a <a href="/wiki/Master_of_Science_in_Information_Technology" title="Master of Science in Information Technology">Master of Science in Information Technology</a> (M.Sc IT) and sometimes continuing onto a doctoral program and earning a <a href="/wiki/Doctorate" title="Doctorate">doctorate</a> such as a <a href="/wiki/Doctor_of_Information_Technology" title="Doctor of Information Technology">Doctor of Information Technology</a> (DIT).</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_variations">International variations</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bachelor_of_Science_in_Information_Technology&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: International variations">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Australia">Australia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bachelor_of_Science_in_Information_Technology&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Australia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a>, <i>Bachelor of Information Technology/Science</i> (BInfTech) programs are three to four years in duration. Honors awarded to graduates who successfully complete a four-year program.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Belgium">Belgium</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bachelor_of_Science_in_Information_Technology&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Belgium">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/Belgium" title="Belgium">Belgium</a>, the <i>Bachelor of Science in Information Technology</i> is a 3-year degree after the compulsory education, but outside the universities; with specializing in certain fields, usually databases, network, realtime operating systems and/or web design. The graduated students can join a postgraduate cycle in order to get a Master of Science in Information Technology degree, this time in the university system.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Bangladesh">Bangladesh</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bachelor_of_Science_in_Information_Technology&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Bangladesh">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In Bangladesh, the <i>Bachelor of Engineering in Information Technology</i> is awarded following a four-year course of study under the Dhaka University,Jahangirnagar University, university of information technology and sciences <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> and Stamford University Bangladesh.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Canada">Canada</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bachelor_of_Science_in_Information_Technology&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Canada">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>, the <i>Bachelor of Science (B.S.) program in Information Technology</i> (IT) with a minor in <i>Business Administration</i> offers an interdisciplinary curriculum focusing on both information technology and business administration. In addition, the program is unique in that it merges traditional academic topics with leading edge and current IT practices and technology. This program is offered under the written consent of the <a href="/wiki/Higher_education_in_British_Columbia" title="Higher education in British Columbia">British Columbia Ministry</a> of Advanced Education.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Germany">Germany</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bachelor_of_Science_in_Information_Technology&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Germany">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>, <i>Bachelor of Science in Information Technology</i> integrates a professional degree in information technology with a major in another country or culture and its language, enhancing professional training and career options. The course is of three to five years duration. Students spend two semesters of study at a university or other higher education institution in the country of their major.</p>
<p>The information technology component provides a sound education in all aspects of computing and information technology for a career in the profession.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="India">India</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bachelor_of_Science_in_Information_Technology&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: India">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a>, a <i>Bachelor of Science in Information Technology</i>(BSc IT) is a 3-year undergraduate program. The Bachelor of Information Science degree is a distinctly different from a general B. Sc. The Bachelor of Engineering in Information Technology, however is a 4-year program and the degree awarded is referred to as B.Tech or B.E.</p>
<p>One can apply for BSc IT after completing <a href="/wiki/Higher_Secondary_School_Certificate" title="Higher Secondary School Certificate">HSC</a> or after completing <a href="/wiki/Diploma_in_Engineering" title="Diploma in Engineering">Engineering Diploma</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Malaysia">Malaysia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bachelor_of_Science_in_Information_Technology&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Malaysia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/Malaysia" title="Malaysia">Malaysia</a> information Technology normally 6 semester or (3 year) with one semester (6 months) for industry training.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Netherlands">Netherlands</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bachelor_of_Science_in_Information_Technology&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Netherlands">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/Netherlands" title="Netherlands">Netherlands</a>, the <i>Bachelor of Science in Information Technology</i> degree is awarded after four years of study with specializing in a certain field.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Namibia">Namibia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bachelor_of_Science_in_Information_Technology&amp;action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Namibia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/Namibia" title="Namibia">Namibia</a>, <i>Bachelor of Science in Information Technology</i> degrees are awarded after three and four years with specialization in areas such as Business Computing, System Administration &amp; Networks as well as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Software_Development" title="Software Development">Software Development</a>. The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Polytechnic_of_Namibia" title="Polytechnic of Namibia">Polytechnic of Namibia</a>, University of Namibia and other educational institutions in Namibia are key producers of graduates in this field.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Nepal">Nepal</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bachelor_of_Science_in_Information_Technology&amp;action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Nepal">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/Nepal" title="Nepal">Nepal</a>, <i>Bachelor of Science in Computer Science</i> and <i>Information Technology</i> (B.Sc.CSIT ) is a four-year course of study.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> The Bachelor of Computer Science and Information Technology is provided by Tribhuvan University and the degree awarded is referred to as BScCSIT.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Pakistan">Pakistan</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bachelor_of_Science_in_Information_Technology&amp;action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Pakistan">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/Pakistan" title="Pakistan">Pakistan</a>, a <i>Bachelor of Science in Information Technology</i> (BS-IT) is 4-year program. Some universities also named as Bachelor of Engineering in Information Technology (BE-IT).BEIT was started in Dr.A.Q Khan institute of Computer Sciences and Information Technology(KICSIT) from 2001 to 2013.Now it has been converted into BSIT.www.kicsit.edu.pk site for Visit.PUCIT is the only university who's ranked 'W'(highest)by HEC in BS(IT) among Pakistan universities.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Philippines">Philippines</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bachelor_of_Science_in_Information_Technology&amp;action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Philippines">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/Philippines" title="Philippines">Philippines</a>, BSIT program normally takes 4 years to complete. Schools with trimester system has less time to complete this course. A total number of 486 hours was set by the <a href="/wiki/Commission_on_Higher_Education_(Philippines)" title="Commission on Higher Education (Philippines)">CHED</a> during internships of the program. <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>On May 4, 2000, the <a href="/wiki/ILOVEYOU" title="ILOVEYOU">ILOVEYOU</a> or Love Bug Virus has spread all over the world affecting millions of computers with an estimated damage of $10 billion.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> The virus was originally a thesis proposal of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Onel_de_Guzman" title="Onel de Guzman">Onel de Guzman</a> which was unanimously rejected by the College of Computer Studies' academic board of <a href="/wiki/AMA_Computer_University" title="AMA Computer University">AMA Computer College Makati</a>, the pioneering IT school in the Philippines.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Portugal">Portugal</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bachelor_of_Science_in_Information_Technology&amp;action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Portugal">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/Portugal" title="Portugal">Portugal</a>, the <i>Bachelor of Science in Information Technology</i> degree is awarded following a ten year course of study without specialization.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Sri_Lanka">Sri Lanka</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bachelor_of_Science_in_Information_Technology&amp;action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Sri Lanka">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/Sri_Lanka" title="Sri Lanka">Sri Lanka</a>, the <i>Bachelor of Science in Information Technology</i> (B.Sc. IT) is either a four-year degree with a specialization, called a major, with honors or a three-year degree without any specialization, called a general degree. <a href="/wiki/University_of_Colombo_School_of_Computing" title="University of Colombo School of Computing">University of Colombo</a> has offered <i>Bachelor of Science in Information and Communication Technology</i> and later it was changed as <i>Bachelor of Science in Information Systems</i>. <a href="/wiki/University_of_Moratuwa" title="University of Moratuwa">University of Moratuwa</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sri_Lanka_Institute_of_Information_Technology" title="Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology">Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology</a> also offers IT degrees. University of Moratuwa gives B.Sc.(Hons) Information Technology 4-year degree.Sri Lanka's first IT faculty was in <a href="/wiki/University_of_Moratuwa" title="University of Moratuwa">University of Moratuwa</a>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="United_States">United States</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bachelor_of_Science_in_Information_Technology&amp;action=edit&amp;section=17" title="Edit section: United States">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>, a B.S. in Information Technology is awarded after a four-year course of study. Some degree programs are accredited by the Computing Accreditation Commission of the Accreditation Board for Engineering and Technology (<a href="/wiki/ABET" title="ABET">ABET</a>).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-toi_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-toi-10">[10]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
