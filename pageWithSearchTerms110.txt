<p><b>Robert Cecil Martin</b> (colloquially known as <b>Uncle Bob</b><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-InformIT_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-InformIT-2">[2]</a></sup>) is an American <a href="/wiki/Software_engineer" title="Software engineer">software engineer</a> and author. He is a co-author of the <a href="/wiki/Agile_software_development#The_Agile_Manifesto" title="Agile software development">Agile Manifesto</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> He now runs a consulting firm called <a class="external text" href="https://sites.google.com/site/unclebobconsultingllc/" rel="nofollow">Clean Coder</a>.</p>
