<p><b><a href="/wiki/Software_engineering" title="Software engineering">Software engineering</a></b> is the application of a systematic, disciplined, quantifiable approach to the design, development, operation, and maintenance of software, and the study of these approaches; that is, the application of engineering to software.</p>
<div class="hatnote relarticle mainarticle" role="note">The main article for this <a href="/wiki/Help:Categories" title="Help:Categories">category</a> is <b><a href="/wiki/Software_engineering" title="Software engineering">Software engineering</a></b>.</div>
<p>See <a href="/wiki/Category:Systems_engineering" title="Category:Systems engineering">Category:Systems engineering</a> for such topics as: <b>Requirements analysis, Systems analysis, Systems architecture, System life cycle,</b> and many more.</p>
